# Privacy policy — Ecosystem Status Hub

**Last updated:** March 31, 2026

## Summary

Ecosystem Status Hub is a **link directory**. It shows public URLs you can open in your browser. It does **not** collect, store, or transmit personal information. It does **not** access or analyze your network traffic, browsing history, or page content.

## Data collection

We do **not** collect any data through this extension.

## Third-party sites

When you click a link, your browser opens that website under **that site’s** own terms and privacy policy. We do not control those sites.

## Contact

For privacy questions about this extension, open an issue on the project repository:  
https://github.com/mapicallo/ecosystem-status-hub

## Changes

We may update this policy if the extension’s behavior changes. The date at the top will reflect the latest revision.
